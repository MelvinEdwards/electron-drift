I am a PhD student at Wayne State University in Detroit Michigan USA. My research focuses on the implementation of various artificial neural networks on-chip in the analog domain. The primary focus is edge applications for IoT. My research also focuses heavily on mixed signal CMOS design to accomplish this task. I have extensive experience with Python, MATLAB, and C++ coding. I am experienced with the applications of DNNs, CNNs, RNNs, and combinations of these architectures to successfully model the underlying statistical distribution of a given dataset. I also have extensive experience in the automotive industry with much of my knowledge on lab equipment and spectral analysis.
